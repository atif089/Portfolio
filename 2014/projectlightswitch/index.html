About

Project Light Switch has been designed to support who you are in the journey to becoming who you want to be.

No one's journey is easy, nor will they share the same experiences and goals. Rather than see that as a challenge we have designed our programs to cater to those dierences. Project Light Switch aims to gather a free resource open guidebook on each individual's journey towards personal growth and living journal of other peoples' experiences, their hardships and their successes, and everything they've learned along the way.
